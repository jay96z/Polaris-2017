Technovision 10.0
A vision beyond horizon

Technovision 10.0, a national level technical festival, is a part of initiative taken by the college with Technical Club of RCOEM to give an opportunity to budding engineers to implement their technical knowledge and explore their creativity. Around 1000 students participate in Technovision. Late Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam, former President of India had inaugurated Technovision 6.0 held in the year 2010.

The tenth edition of this technical extravaganza comes with the aim of kindling creativity, innovation and motivating the students to indulge in technical activities.

About Technical club

With a motive to provide a common platform for Interdisciplinary Technical Activities of the College, the Technical Club was launched in 2015. The Club aims to technically strengthen the students by integrating their skills in the various fields of engineering & technology, so as to cope up with the highly competitive environment.

The Club carriers out its activities through students coordinators who function under the supervision of the Professor Incharge of the Club Dr. Rajeshree. D. Raut.

Clubs/Units under Technical Club:
1. AutoTronix Club
2. Competitive Exam Unit
3. RoboTech Club
4. Robotics Club
5. Embedded Club

About the Institute

RCOEM, situated in the heart of India in Nagpur city, was established in 1984 by Shri Ramdeobaba Sarvajanik Samiti, a trust which has been involved in community service for over four decades. 33 years of existence has helped RCOEM grow deep roots and establish a strong foundation in technical education. Journey of a student in this institute has always involved comprehensive knowledge building from practical skills, theoretical knowledge to personality development, which has given them a head-start in their careers.

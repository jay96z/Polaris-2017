CODE-O-MANIA

It is a multilanguage programming competition where you can test your programming skills.

MIND THE LINE

Mind the Line is the robo racing event along the certain path, which check the the accuracy and speed of the robot.

JUNKYARD WARS

Junkyard Wars is an event where the participants will have to work as a team and create a machine or a mechanism from the scrap provided.

PAPERFEST

It is an event that dilates the scope for engineers to present various aspects of electronic design,software/shardware,electrical and mechanical technologies.

BREADBOARD MAZE

This competition has three rounds- aptitude round, circuit design round and complex circuit design.

AQUABOT

Aquabot is a water robot event where the partcipants would bring their self made robots,which should float,pick objects and place them on position.

TECH-QUIZ

The competition is going to be based on Basic Knowledge; the set of questions will be related to the general academic curriculum to make it more practicable for participants.

ROBOWARS

Ever wondered what happens when formidable bots compete with each other? It will be a war every human aspires to watch.

TECH-QUEST

As the name suggests in this event the participants will have to identify the clue and reach the next location.

IMPULSE

Impulse is an Internal Combustion- Radio Control (IC-RC) Car racing event where in the participants would be bringing their self made IC-RC Cars and competing with other participants in the event.

CRAZE FOR CAD

Craze for cad is an complete AutoCAD event where different levels of AutoCAD starting from 2D and ends at 3D in which participants compete with each other.

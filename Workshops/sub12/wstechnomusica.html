  • TECHNO-MUSICA

    Music has always been an integral part of our Life. Have we really given a thought about the science behind it? In the Journey from ‘Harmonium to Synthesizers’, Electronics and Engineering has a valuable contribution. This Workshop gives you a chance to understand the science of music and an interesting blend of Music with Engineering. Explore the Alluring World of music through Engineers Eye

  • Session 1: AUDIO SIGNAL AND ITS PROCESSING

    • What is an audio signal?

    • Basics of Digital sound

    • Basic concepts of DSP

    • Reading a spectrum and spectrogram

    • Filters


    Session 2: MUSIC THEORY

    • How is music created

    • Consonant, Dissonant and frequencies

    • Theory of Musical notes (Hindustani and Western both)

    • How Instruments are designed

    • Role of Electronics in Musical Instruments


    Session 3: TECHNOLOGY IN MUSIC

    • Role of Electronics in MUSIC

    • Basics of Audio Engineering

    • Earsketch

    • Music through Arduino

    • Music Production

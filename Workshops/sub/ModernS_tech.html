  • MODERN SURVEYING TECHNIQUES

     

  • This workshop has been organised to bridge the gap between theoretical and practical knowledge about engineering survey.
    Its prime objective is to create awareness of modern surveying technics like EDM(Electronic distance measurement) instrument like total station and GIS (geographical information system).

    Workshop Benefits & Highlights:

    • This workshop has been organised to bridge the gap between theoretical and practical knowledge about engineering survey.
    • A live DEMONSTRATION OF latest and expensive instruments like "Total Station" and software of GIS like Google earth and ARCH GIS,
    • ARCH GIS is worth of 13 lach; no other institute of apart from vnit has it and working on is usp
    • The participant will be guided by the field experts with more than 25 years of experience in modern surveying
